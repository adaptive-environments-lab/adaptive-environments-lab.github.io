# Adaptive Environments Lab

AI-enabled environmental design research and teaching at the University of Virginia.

**Website:** [adaptiveenvironmentslab.org](https://adaptiveenvironmentslab.org)

## About

The Adaptive Environments Lab is a collaboration between UVA's School of Medicine and School of Architecture, focused on democratizing sophisticated spatial analysis for designers across disciplines.
